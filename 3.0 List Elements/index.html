Angela's Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • C¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter

    For the filling:

  1. 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  2. ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  3. ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  4. ¼ cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  3. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  4. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  5. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
